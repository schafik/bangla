loans: overview

Do you currently have any loans outstanding? By loans I mean any amount money borrowed from a bank, employer, moneylender, family member or credit association, credit purchase, or any other money you owe back.

What is the total amount of the loans you currently have outstanding including the amount of the interest (~1300 NAs)?

Compared to one year ago, is the loan amount that you currently owe __? (~1300 NAs)

10 step ladder

Thinking about the amount of money that you borrowed from friends, family or taken as a loan from a bank, moneylender or other source. To what extent do you and your spouse agree on the amount of money you decide to borrow? Step 1 is disagree completely. Step 10 is agree completely. (~1500 NAs)
